Some most visited places in India

Varanasi

"The spiritual capital of india"

World's oldest living city, Varanasi - also known as Kashi (City of Life) and Benaras, is the spiritual capital of India. It is one of Hinduism's seven holy cities. The old city of Varanasi lies along the western banks of the Ganges, spread across a labyrinth of narrow galis.

Known For : Ganga Aarti, Dashashwamedh Ghat

Jaipur

"The pink city"

Also called the Pink City, Jaipur is the capital of the royal state of Rajasthan. Along with Delhi and Agra, Jaipur forms the Golden Triangle and hails as one of the most famous tourist circuits in the country.Rajasthan is one of the most colourful and flashiest states in all of India.

Known For : Amer Fort, Chokhi Dhani, City Palace

Goa

"Beaches, Sunsets and Crazy Nights"

Goa is the epitome of tourism in India with one of the best beaches, food and nightlife. It is getting popular among foreigners as well. When you think of Goa, you think of sandy beaches, amazing parties, beautiful little villages and a magical holiday experience.

Known For : Calangute Beach, Fort Aguada
